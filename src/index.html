Are there any limits during the free trial?
Reasonable use limits apply. If you exceed 200 minutes during the trial, you will be billed a per-minute charge corresponding to the locality of your number.

In certain countries, you may have to pay a small fee to provision a new number. You will see a breakdown of additional fees you may incur outside the U.S. when you set up your free trial.

Will Context still work if I want to forward calls to a landline?
When you forward calls to a landline, the context for the call will be delivered to your cell phone or the web app. You will have to be logged in to see the contextual data as your phone rings.
